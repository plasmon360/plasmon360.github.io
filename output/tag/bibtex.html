Bibtex in Word

Posted on Thu 07 January 2010 in Free Software, Latex, Publications • Tagged with bibtex, Latex, word, writing

Bibtex4Word is an add-in for Microsoft Word that allows the citation of references and the insertion of a bibliography into word document. This add-in imbibes all the good things of bibtex in latex documents.More information is available onBibtex4word website.
