The two terms in the electric field consists of 1) near field (area near to the dipole) and 2) far field (area far from the dipole) contributions. Far field falls of as [tex]\frac{1}{r}[/tex] and the near field falls of by [tex]\frac{1}{r\^3}[/tex].
