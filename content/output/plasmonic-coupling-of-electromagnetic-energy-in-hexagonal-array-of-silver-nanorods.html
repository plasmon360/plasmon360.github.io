Plasmonic coupling of electromagnetic energy in hexagonal array of silver nanorods

Published: Fri 08 January 2010
By juluribk

In Plasmonics.

tags: FDTD MEEP Plasmonics

A cool animation which I reproduced from W.M Saj, Optics Express, Vol 13, 13, 2005 using freely obtained FDTD software (MEEP). It demonstrates the propagation of electromagnetic energy in hexagonal array of silver nanorods due to plasmonic coupling. The source is offset from the center and the energy is propagated in an interesting snake like pattern.
