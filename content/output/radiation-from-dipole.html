Radiation from an oscillating dipole

In Free Software, Matlab, Plasmonics.

tags: electromagnetism Matlab Plasmonics

The electric field from an oscillating dipole is given by:, where [tex]\hat{r}[/tex] is the position vector, [tex] \omega [/tex] is the frequency of dipole oscillation, [tex]\textbf{p}[/tex] is the dipole moment. The two terms in the electric field consists of 1) near field (area near to the dipole) and 2) far field (area far from the dipole) contributions. Far field falls of as [tex]\frac{1}{r}[/tex] and the near field falls of by [tex]\frac{1}{r\^3}[/tex].
